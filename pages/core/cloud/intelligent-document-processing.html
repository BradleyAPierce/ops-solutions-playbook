With Dispatcher ScanTrip Cloud, workflows are managed in an easy-to-use cloud interface. Documents scanned at the MFP can be automatically analyzed for important data, such as title and date using Advanced OCR and automatically renamed based on gathered data. Vital metadata is saved for export; processed documents and associated metadata are automatically saved to the appropriate folder for storage.  You can also browse your directory structure to easily choose a folder to store your documents with type-ahead functionality to quickly filter the list of folders using the visually enhanced display at the MFP. 

ScanTrip Cloud is a perfect door-opener for new customers, allowing them to start experiencing the benefits of cloud-based workflow automation. As their needs grow over time, customers can take advantage of a streamlined upgrade to a full Dispatcher Stratus license or add Release2Me Cloud for secure print release in the cloud. 

Benefits

  • Kick-start digital transformation with pre-built scan workflows
  • All-in-one security platform for print and scan including bizhub SECURE Notifier
  • Perform frequently used scan applications simply & efficiently with built in authentication
  • Easily change send options and/or preview field before transmitting
  • Customize workflows to include PDF conversion, document renaming, and more

Dispatcher Stratus is a cloud workflow solution that is designed to streamline and consolidate your organization’s processes by incorporating People-Based Workflows
into Intelligent Document Processing (IDP). That means the processes that can be automated are automated, and the processes that require a human touch are simple to accomplish. Using Dispatcher Stratus, customers can manage their workflows, users, devices, forms, authentication, and more from a single location that is accessible from anywhere. Dispatcher Stratus is designed to match an organization’s needs exactly. Easily add, group, and remove employees, devices, workflows, and forms as needs change.

Boost Productivity:
Dispatcher Stratus allows organizations to send jobs to individuals, groups, or queues, ensuring that every job is made available to the right employee. The Jobs interface provides vital information, including areas for document previews, notes, job history, and more.

Maximize Existing Resources
Dispatcher Stratus takes advantage of your existing hardware and connects to the software and cloud storage solutions you’re already using. Most importantly, it frees your employees from time-consuming manual tasks and lets them focus on growing your business.

Automation at your Fingertips
Dispatcher Stratus offers many advanced processing capabilities to ensure your documents are renamed, annotated, modified, and converted the way your organization needs. Additional features include connections to ODBC-compliant databases, metadata manipulation with LUA scripting, and more.

Web Forms Process Management
Dispatcher Stratus supports custom form creation and management, meaning that your workflows are even more accessible than ever before. Documents and data can be captured from native form submissions that minimize dependencies on inefficient paper forms and PDFs. Forms can be branded or embedded within your existing website and can be tracked and interacted with from the Dispatcher Stratus portal.

Benefits

  • Allow employees to interact with documents and data as they progress through workflows
  • Intelligently capture paper-based or electronic files, as well as form data, into any workflow
  • Drive collaboration by ensuring your documents are available when and where personnel need them
  • Provide an easy-to-use and secure solution for indexing business-critical data using invoices, HR forms, contracts, and more
  • Customize workflows and forms to meet your business needs with intuitive Workflow Designer and Form Builder tools
