Netaphor Site Audit

Netaphor Site Audit is a fleet monitoring and fleet management tool that captures and provides valuable device data across mixed Printer/MFP fleets. It provides a complete set of assessment tools for all needs, from small business to enterprise, general office to secure environments. The tool set includes floor mapping and TCO tools to show an optimal future state. SiteAudit software is ideal for all businesses, from SME to enterprise, and helps support fleet-related activities.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive Network Visibility - full audit of all devices and systems across the network
  • Identifies Security Risks and Gaps - highlights potential security exposures like unmanaged devices, outdated firmware, or unauthorized endpoints
  • Improves Infrastructure Planning - detailed asset and traffic analysis to make more informed decisions around upgrading hardware, optimizing configurations, or consolidating systems
  • Accelerates Compliance and Reporting - standardized reports help meet internal and external compliance requirements
