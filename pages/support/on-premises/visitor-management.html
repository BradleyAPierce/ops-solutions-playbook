School Gate Guardian (SGG) is a visitor management check-in solution. The software provides instantaneous sex-offender background checks, emergency mass alerts, and web based reporting among other notable features. Their add-on solutions include Volunteer Management Check-In, Attendance Check-In, RSVP, and Digital Hall Pass solutions.

The visitor management software is now available on-premises or as a cloud-hosted solution. This hybrid software as a Service (SAAS) solution gives you the flexibility to host data on your own network or in a secured hosted environment.

There are three versions of the software available on-premises or as a hosted solution:

School Gate Guardian Pro Operator Bundle – Includes SGG terminal software, Card Scanning Solutions 800DX duplex ID scanner, Dymo 450 Turbo thermal badge printer, Code Reader bar-code reader, Microsoft Lifecam webcam, unlimited sex offender look-ups, installed on a district provided PC.

School Gate Guardian Self-Serve Bundle – Includes SGG Self-Serve software, SGG Pro software, AssureTec ID-150 duplex ID scanner, webcams, thermal badge printers, unlimited sex offender look-ups, installed on a district provided PC.

School Gate Guardian Self-Serve Kiosk – Includes all software and hardware components enclosed in an attractive kiosk for self-service visitor check-in.

Benefits of the On-Premises Solution:

  • Database and reporting are hosted on your secured network
  • Communication between application and database stays on your network
  • You are in complete control of your environment

Benefits of the Cloud-Hosted Solution:

  • Services hosted on secured servers/network
  • You do not need to maintain a server
  • School Gate Guardian maintains your database, backups, and security updates
  • Minimal to no impact on your IT
