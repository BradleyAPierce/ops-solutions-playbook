Tungsten ControlSuite (AutoStore)

Whether you're handling invoices, claims, applications or order forms, user errors in document processing can be costly to your business. With AutoStore, you'll automate your document-driven business processes to reduce costs, improve operational efficiency and support regulatory compliance.

How AutoStore can help:

  • Broad multi-vendor MFP support Create a consistent user experience across devices. AutoStore works with all major office multifunction printer (MFP) manufacturers.
  • Multichannel support Support a wide range of document and information capture channels, enabling distributed employees to work how, when and where they need.
  • Bi-directional database lookups Easily search and retrieve database information for display on any device.
  • System connectors Seamlessly connect to 40-plus enterprise software applications and mobile devices, increasing the volume and accuracy of information that flows into those systems.
  • Native LDAP integration Browse your company contact list or search by domain name to return a selectable list of addresses from any supported MFP.
  • Tungsten Equitrac integration Integrate with the Tungsten Equitrac® print management and cost recovery solution and enable activity tracking and unified authentication, from any supported MFP.
  • Advanced capture Dynamically apply security and compliance rules based on content to trigger associated document workflows.

Benefits

  • Automated content-based workflow
  • Quickly and accurately share information and collaborate with colleagues and customers in an efficient and centralized way.
  • Unlock document intelligence with content-aware capture workflow automation to ensure security and compliance.
