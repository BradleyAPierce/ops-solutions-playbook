OpenText Fax

OpenText Fax (formerly known as RightFax) is a scalable digital fax solution that replaces traditional faxing methods. It allows users to securely exchange faxes without the need for physical fax machines, integrating seamlessly with enterprise applications like SAP and Oracle.

Key features include:

Secure Paperless Faxing: Enables secure transmission of faxes from email or desktop applications.

Integration: Works with various industry applications to enhance business processes.

Flexibility and Reliability: Offers advanced security and automation capabilities, making it a trusted choice for enterprises.

Streamlined Communications: Digitizes the fax process to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Benefits

  • Eliminates inefficiencies, drives competitive differentiation and improves the bottom line
  • Reduces cost and maximizes productivity by digitizing manual, paper-based information exchange
  • Increases productivity by integrating with email and desktop applications
  • Ensures compliance with customer, industry and data privacy mandates
  • The bEST certified OpenText Fax Connector provides a direct link from the compatible bizhub MFP control panel to the OpenText Fax Server
