Security Features of the Hosted Solution:
• Security starts with clustered firewalls to help filter out unwanted traffic
• Network monitoring watches for anything odd and alerts hosting administrators
• All data sent to and from servers are sent via an encrypted 256-bit SSL (TLS 1.2) channel
• Servers are scanned and locked down according to PCI DSS 3.1
• Data communication between internal web service and database servers are encrypted via a separate 256-bit SSL (TLS 1.2) channel
• Database data is encrypted at rest, meaning your data is always protected
• All TLS certificates are constantly, and automatically, kept up to date to make sure communications are always using the latest encryption
• Third party environmental and security monitoring
• Each customer has their own database and web services, and are secured with their own authentication

Dispatcher Phoenix for Education:

Konica Minolta's Dispatcher Phoenix optional Education Package is designed for K-12 schools along with colleges and universities. With Dispatcher Phoenix, educators and administrators alike can automate repetitive manual tasks, share admission applications quickly and easily with a direct connection to popular cloud storage service providers, convert educational documents to searchable and editable text, and easily index student records at the MFP panel. Dispatcher Phoenix simplifies document processing for all types of educational institutions – from K-12 to colleges and universities.

Key features in K-12 schools, colleges, and universities include:

  • Generate Tests, Grade Results, and Assess Progress. Dispatcher Phoenix for educators includes a Bubble Grader feature that lets teachers quickly and easily print their own bubble sheet tests on plain paper, from bizhub® and other MFPs – then scan and grade results automatically, with data reports at their fingertips in minutes. With this automated scoring and assessment solution, teachers will be able to spend more time in the classroom focusing on what's really important – educating their students.
  • Web-Tools for Anytime Access to Bubble Sheet Generation. Using Dispatcher Phoenix, educators can seamlessly create and manage Bubble Sheet test templates right from an intuitive web interface. Educators can quickly and easily apply student rosters to existing templates to streamline test creation or save templates for future use. This web-based tool allows teachers to configure custom exam details including the number of questions, a number of answers for questions, enables multiple answers per question and create unique answer keys to prevent fraudulent test results while protecting sensitive student data. In addition, educators can easily add weights to specific questions when creating bubble sheet tests. This feature allows educators to assign point values to individual test questions according to the level of importance they determine.
  • Edit Documents After Scanning. Searching through or modifying student records and other paperwork is easy with Dispatcher Phoenix. Your scanned documents can be automatically converted to editable and searchable formats, such as text, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, PDF Searchable, PDF/A for archiving, etc.
  • Share Academic Information.  Index, scan and store your paper-based curriculum resources and class materials into Microsoft SharePoint and SharePoint Online. These connectors allow you to log into your Microsoft SharePoint or SharePoint Online account, choose the appropriate SharePoint library, index your document for future retrieval and upload your scanned documents into SharePoint – directly at the MFP or via an automated workflow. Dispatcher Phoenix also includes connectors to cloud storage services, such as Google Drive, Box, Dropbox, FilesAnywhere, FileAssist and much more.
  • Intelligent Redaction. The Dispatcher Phoenix Education package offers intelligent redaction, highlight and strikeout features that allow you to permanently black out, strike out or highlight text with a single click. Redacted text cannot be retrieved by searching after documents have been exported, and redaction text can be updated right at the MFP.

  • Intelligent redaction to safeguard and secure student records.
  • Advanced OCR to automatically extract key information from student transcripts.
  • Connectors to popular cloud storage service providers, such as Google Drive, Box, Dropbox, Microsoft's OneDrive and OneDrive for Business, WebDAV, FilesAnywhere, and Konica Minolta's FileAssist.
