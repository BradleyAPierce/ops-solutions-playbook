Dispatcher Phoenix for Legal

Dispatcher Phoenix's optional Legal Package is Konica Minolta's unique solution for legal document handling, streamlining the preparation of case files, automating time-consuming manual processing tasks such as Bates stamping and redaction, and increasing productivity for legal service providers. With its rules-based distribution, documents can be sent to different destinations in a single step. And optional connectors allow you to scan, index, process and upload case files directly into popular document management systems, such as Worldox and Workshare.

Key features for law firms and other legal customers include:

File Conversion. Because text searching is essential to the discovery process, Dispatcher Phoenix Legal makes OCR conversion quick and easy. You can convert files to a variety of PDF formats, including PDF, Searchable PDF, PDF/A and Searchable PDF/A.

Bates Stamping. You can identify pages with Bates Stamping, placing sequential numbers and/or date/time marks on documents as they are scanned or processed – for example, during the discovery stage of preparations for trial. You can add multiple Bates stamps to each page in various positions and customize Bates stamp text with advanced formatting options and identifying marks that enable attorneys and researchers to keep track of materials more easily.

Intelligent Redaction. Dispatcher Phoenix Legal package offers intelligent redaction, highlight and strikeout features that allow you to permanently black out, strike out or highlight text with a single click – and redacted text cannot be retrieved by searching after documents have been exported.
