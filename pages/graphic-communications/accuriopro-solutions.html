The AccurioPro Color Manager (APCM) Suite is a comprehensive set of tools designed to linearize, calibrate, profile, validate, and control the color quality of printing presses. This unique and all-encompassing suite makes APCM an unparalleled offering in the world of color management and control. Its user-optimized interface and wizard-based workflow interaction make the software simple and easy to use, even for inexperienced users, reducing learning curves for operators. Additionally, the APCM Suite includes robust print job tracking capabilities, allowing operators to monitor the press performance over time. Ultimately, the streamlined processes and flawless operation will maximize the output of your printing presses, enhance workflows, and increase the efficiency of your print room. By ensuring consistent and accurate color quality, the APCM Suite helps businesses deliver high-quality print products, improve customer satisfaction, and optimize their print room operations.

APCM Print is a standalone application paired with the IQ-501 that eliminates the need for tedious color fine-tuning to meet customer expectations by simplifying the process. The solution scans the customer’s printed sample and prints the file through the engine glass, then calibrates the colors using the IQ-501 to create a customized device link profile for that specific job. This ensures that the original colors are matched as closely as possible without requiring further manual adjustments.

Benefits

  • Advanced Color Control: APCM Suite offers comprehensive tools for linearization, calibration, profiling, and validation—ensuring precise, repeatable color across all presses
  • Operator-Friendly Efficiency: Guided workflows and an intuitive interface reduce training time and improve performance, even for less experienced users
  • Automated Job-Specific Calibration: APCM Print with IQ-501 scans samples and auto-adjusts color—eliminating manual tweaks and improving consistency

AccurioPro SECURE provides robust protection for corporate data and documents, which is essential for maintaining a company’s financial health and reputation. This service can be activated on any Konica Minolta professional print device, offering flexibility regardless of business size.

Organizations in finance, government, healthcare, and legal sectors often handle sensitive information that requires stringent security measures. By integrating AccurioPro SECURE, these sectors can ensure their documents and data are safeguarded against potential breaches, thereby protecting their finances and reputation. The diverse authentication options further enhance its suitability for various environments.

Benefits

  • Comprehensive Data Protection AccurioPro Secure encrypts the entire hard drive of your professional print device, ensuring that sensitive business data is protected—even if the drive is physically removed
  • Automatic Data Overwrite & Deletion It eliminates traces of deleted data through temporary data overwrite and can be configured to automatically delete stored print jobs, reducing the risk of unauthorized access
  • Secure Password Management The system enforces strong password policies by requiring changes to default administrator passwords and enabling secure alphanumeric combinations, helping prevent unauthorized access
  • Regulatory Compliance & Peace of Mind Designed to meet stringent data protection standards like GDPR, AccurioPro Secure helps businesses—especially SMBs—stay compliant while safeguarding their reputation against data breaches
