PREFACE

This portfolio is intended to provide you with an overview of the diverse solutions available to you from Konica Minolta.

The current solutions catalog is overflowing with beneficial solutions,  but has minimal organization. Too many solutions create saturation within the sales organization and leads to confusion on what KMBS should lead with. To alleviate this situation, the solutions catalog, along with this portfolio, have been categorized according to the following classifications:

• Core Solutions – Cloud and On-Premises

• Supported Solutions – Cloud and On-Premises

• Vertical Solutions

• Graphic Communications

• The Core solutions have high sales volume, significant profit and a high degree of Konica Minolta professional services support capability. It is also desirable for the solution to be Konica Minolta’s own IP. Communication for these solutions will include but are not limited to promotions and educational events.

• The Supported solutions possess low to high sales volume and profit with mid-range professional services support capability. These will generally be third party solutions. Field communication will be kept to a minimum and grouped together to allow field focus to remain on the Core and Next New solutions. Some of the Supported Solutions have a large installed base and are still very desirable by many mid to large size businesses and organizations.

• The Vertical solutions pull the best selling solutions in each vertical.

• The Graphic Communications discuss the solutions that pair with industrial print production and reprographics centers.

• Solutions product classifications are also outlined in the solutions pricebook.

Due to the informative nature of this document, and the continual updates to the solutions Konica Minolta offers in an effort to provide you with the solutions that will best meet the demanding needs of today’s customers, we have not included Solution Item Numbers or Solution Item Descriptions.

Please refer to Price Books/Price Sheets on mykonicaminolta.com for pricing information, solution description and solution item numbers.
