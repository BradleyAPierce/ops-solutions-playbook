bizhub SECURE

How can you make certain your valuable data is SAFE from THEFT – and prevent it from being stolen from your MFP or extracted if the hard disk drive is removed from your device? The answer is simple – and all it takes is allowing an authorized Konica Minolta Field Engineer to activate the bizhub security features on your bizhub MFP. With bizhub Secure safeguards in place, you will know that your documents have uncompromising security protection.

bizhub SECURE Platinum

The bizhub SECURE Platinum Service was developed to provide resources needed to lock down and protect any document data that might reside on the bizhub’s internal hard drive. To secure and protect any document image data that might be present on the hard drive, the following security functions can be enabled:

• Hard Drive Encryption*
• Hard Drive Lock Password
• Automatic deletion of any temporary image data
• Data overwrite of electronic documents on a timed basis
Ultimately, protecting student information is everyone’s responsibility. Konica Minolta has led the industry in providing enhanced security features for the digital era – and with powerful bizhub SECURE Platinum functions activated by the Konica Minolta Field Engineer, it provides an additional line of defense against data theft and unauthorized access to educational documents or devices.

 

bizhub SECURE Healthcare 

ADVANCED SECURITY SOLUTIONS TO MEET THE CHALLENGES OF PATIENT CONFIDENTIALITY.

How can healthcare providers protect patient confidentiality? Are they encrypting medical records or protecting files using passwords? Does access to their printer/ copier require user authentication? Are they tracking print/copy functions so they know who, when and where copies were made? Is their email traffic logged and preserved? Are printouts and faxes containing PHI protected from unauthorized personnel? Have they extended their network security protocol to their multifunction devices? And are they confident that HIPAA compliance measures are in place at every step of your medical workflow?

This is all covered with bizhub SECURE Healthcare. This solution and IT management benefits combine to enhance healthcare provider’s security, speed their work and help control their costs.

  • Create a 20-digit secure alphanumeric password to lock down your bizhub storage media 
  • Encrypt the entire contents of your storage media for remarkable data security 
  • Eliminate any trace of data even after it’s been deleted with Temporary Data Overwrite (Temporary Data Overwrite conforms to DoD methods)*
  • Time your bizhub MFP to auto delete any material located in personal or public User Boxes, System User Boxes, Documents and Folders
  • Disable Non-secured and unwanted Services, Protocols and Ports at the MFP
  • Enable TLS v1.3 on the MFP (self-signed certificate)
  • Enable Network User Authentication
  • Enable MFP Audit Logs

*Only available on HDD based models

BreachAlert

Multifunction printers (MFPs) often pose a security risk, as organizations may not be aware of data or document breaches when they occur. Unauthorized use of MFPs by authorized users is a significant source of data breaches, leaving organizations in the dark about who used the MFP, for what purpose, and when. BreachAlert is a unique document and data protection application exclusive to Konica Minolta devices. It provides real-time notifications of data breaches involving Konica Minolta MFPs, helping organizations comply with company, industry, and government privacy and data security requirements. With BreachAlert, organizations can track and monitor documents copied, printed, scanned, faxed, and emailed by individual users. 

In today’s digital age, data security is more critical than ever. BreachAlert ensures that your customers receive immediate alerts in case of potential data breaches, enabling them to take swift action to mitigate risks. 

Breach Alert Notifications 

  • Key word and key data breach triggers; easily and quickly user-configurable 
  • Automatic email notification of breach alerts to user-designated personnel (such as a Compliance Officer) 
  • ·Key data includes SSN, credit & debit card patterns, patient & student numbers, and much more 
  • Create breach and alert severity levels 

MFP Activity Archive 

  • Full-text or keyword searchable archive of all MFP documents printed, copied, scanned, emailed, and faxed 
  • Document auto-archiving and categorizing of all MFP activities 
  • Searchable by day, time, device, user, and much more 
  • Audit trail of all MFP activity for compliance requirement 

Easy Administration 

  • Easy to add and view connected MFPs 
  • Easy to add/modify key words, key data, and alerts 
  • Easy to respond to email breach alerts 
  • Set up notification groups based on roles/user security
