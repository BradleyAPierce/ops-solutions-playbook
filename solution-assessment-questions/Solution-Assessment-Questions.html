SOLUTION ASSESSMENT QUESTIONS

Please refer to vertical playbooks for a complete list of solution assessment questions specific to the education, healthcare and legal vertical markets.

General

  • What are your biggest challenges when it comes to working with documents in the areas of::
    • Is the customer interested in cloud or on-premises solutions?
    • Printing / production?
    • Scanning?
    • Routing?
    • Storage and retrieval?
    • Creating, combining or working with PDF documents?

Device Management

  • Are you looking for device management tools that run on-premises or would you prefer something cloud-based?
  • How are you currently managing your printers and copiers across locations - is it handled by IT, facilities, or a 3rd party?
  • Do you have visibility into usage, toner levels and service alerts across all your devices?
  • How do you handle maintenance or downtime issues when a device goes offline?
  • Is it important to be notified of security settings being altered on the MFP?
  • Do you have a solution that automatically scans, inventories your MFPs?

Document Management

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • Are you currently using Sharepoint or have a WebDev server?
    • How do you get documents into the system today?
  • How are you currently storing your documents today?
  • Do you need to control access to your electronic documents?
  • Do you have a document management system?
    • How do you get documents into the system today?
  • Does your company currently store documents at Recall or Iron Mountain?
  • How often do you need to quickly retrieve a document that is stored?
  • Are those files ever missing or misfiled?
  • How many filing cabinets would you estimate you have?
  • How does your organization handle eDiscovery requests? What document management and/or eDiscovery software is in place to quickly respond to requests for your organizationâ€™s electronic documents during discovery?
  • How do you currently add Bates numbering to your documents? Is it done in-house or outsourced?
  • How do convert your hard copy files to PDF, PDF/A, Word, or Excel?
  • If you are already converting paper documents to searchable digital documents, does the method you use meet your needs?
  • Does the organization have a need to share paper documents between partners and staff in and outside of firm?
  • How do you share electronic records between clients, court, insurance companies or other firms?

Intelligent Document Processing

  • Is the customer interested in cloud or on-premises solutions?
  • How do you create PDF documents today?
  • How do you combine Word, Excel and PowerPoint documents?
  • When you scan documents, what do you do with them after they have been scanned?
  • Do you need to write on them, add notes or mark them up?
  • How often does your company need to convert PDFs to Word or Excel?
  • How do you currently search PDF documents, either for the document itself or within the document?
  • How often do you need to search these PDF documents?
  • What would you like to be able to do with your documents if you could?
  • How do you apply Bates Stamping, perform redactions or perform other editing operations?
  • How do you share and access documents?
  • How do you capture, process and route files?

Fax Solutions

  • Is your customer looking for an on-premises or cloud fax solution?
  • What type of phone lines (analog/digital/VoIP) are utilized in your agency now?
  • How many fax lines do you currently have in your facility?
  • What is your current process for fax management?
  • What have you done to analyze your current spending on fax maintenance (i.e. toner, maintenance fees, paper, fax replacements) vs. other fax options?
  • Does your organization use fax to communicate with outside organizations?
  • What is the average number of faxes you receive on a daily basis?
  • Does your staff often need to re-transmit faxes due to busy signals or complaints about image quality? Do your employees have to wait in line for a fax?
  • When people fax out a document, how do you know if they typed the wrong fax number? What measures have you implemented to mitigate this risk?
  • Does your organization need to provide an audit trail for all fax documents sent and received?

Konica Minolta MarketPlace

  • Are you currently using any workflow or cloud apps directly from your MFP?
  • Do you use cloud storage services like SharePoint or Dropbox?
    • Would it help if your MFP could automatically scan to those cloud platforms?
  • What are the biggest bottlenecks your team experiences when scanning, printing, or distributing documents?
  • How do you currently share files with outside clients and manage versions once they've been shared?
  • Does your email client have an attachment size limit, and if so what do you do to send larger files?
  • What process do your users go through to access the files on devices and how do you secure this process?
  • How do you decide which emails sent by your users should be protected and those which should not? Meaning do you allow file sharing from personal accounts? If you could improve this process how would you do it?

Print Management

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • How does your organization capture costs for the following: research (i.e. legal), scans, prints, copies, faxes, phone calls, courier services, postage or catering?
  • When your staff uses an MFP, how do they capture that use so that the cost can be recovered?
  • How do you integrate your cost recovery with your time and billing system, document management system or litigation support system?
  • How do you handle charging your clients, students, departments, customers, etc. for special printing treatments, such as covers, tabs in documents and bindings?
  • What built-in safeguards does your cost recovery system have to ensure authorized use?
  • What features does your cost recovery system have to help you maintain document confidentiality when printing at any MFP on your network?
  • Are you satisfied that your business is getting the full benefit that your cost recovery system can offer?
  • What would make your cost recovery system better than it is now?
  • Do you have a goal for improving cost recovery? What is the goal? Why is this goal important to you?

Security

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solutions?
  • What is your organizationâ€™s plan for protecting confidentiality of paper-based documents?
  • Can anyone, even a visitor, walk up to your MFPs and copy and/or scan? If so, do you need help with a remedy for this?
  • How does your organization protect sensitive information that is stored on your MFPs from unauthorized access?
  • When archiving digital files, how do you ensure long-term preservation? Are you using a format such as PDF/A?
  • Is your scan and print transfer SSL encrypted?
  • How are you managing the security risks when employees leave confidential print jobs on your network printers?
  • How do you enable your staff to print securely and confidentially at any MFP on your network?
  • Do you work with documents that contain proprietary product information?
  • Do you know what information gets stored on the hard drives of your MFPs?
  • How does your organization ensure that no confidential data remains on the hard drive of the MFP when it leaves your place of business?
  • What are your organizationâ€™s methods for deleting information stored on the hard drives of your MFPs?
  • What was your most recent document security problem? How did you resolve the problem to your satisfaction?

Integrated Option Solutions (bizhub i-Option)

  • Do you need your MFPs to integrate with 3rd party platforms?
  • Do any of your users require accessibility support when operating MFPs, such as visual or cognitive assistance?
    • --If yes, recommend LK-104 Voice Guidance for improved usability through verbal instructions.
  • How often do you need to extract or reuse text from scanned documents or PDFs?
    • -- If frequently, suggest LK-105 OCR Text Recognition or LK-110 Document Converter Pack for searchable PDFs and Office format conversion.
  • Are you printing documents that include barcodes or specialized fonts for financial or enterprise systems like SAP or AS400?
    • -- If yes, consider LK-106 Barcode Fonts, LK-108 OCR A and B Fonts, or LK-107 Unicode Fonts depending on the use case.
  • Is network performance a concern when printing large files across multiple locations or departments?
    • -- If yes, introduce LK-111 ThinPrintÂ® Client to reduce bandwidth usage and improve print speed.
  • Do you require enhanced security features to protect against malware or ensure safe document handling?
    • -- If yes, recommend LK-116 Bitdefender Antivirus for real-time scanning and threat prevention.

Mobile Applications

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • Are you in need of a BYOD (bring your own device) solution for your business in order to manage employee and guest mobile printing needs?
  • Do you want to control who can print from a mobile device as well as control what MFPs/printers are available for mobile printing?
  • Do you want to control who can perform mobile printing based on credentials? Using LDAP? Using other 3rd Party print management or accounting solutions?
  • Are you concerned about the confidentiality of jobs printed from mobile devices? Do you want to ensure you meet security regulations?
  • Do you have a lot of users using mobile devices such as smartphones, tablets or laptops?

PDF Editing

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • Do you often need to edit text or images directly within PDF documents without converting them to another format?
  • Are you currently using manual processes to recreate scanned documents or tables for editing?
  • Do you need to redact sensitive information or apply Bates numbering for legal or compliance purposes?
  • Is your team collaborating on PDFs and needing to add comments, bookmarks, or assemble documents from multiple sources?
  • Do you require automation or batch processing for repetitive PDF tasks like conversion, merging, or annotation?

Pay-For-Print

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • How do you currently manage and recover printing and copying costs across your organization?
  • Do you need to support multiple payment methods such as stored-value cards, mobile pay, credit/debit, or account-based billing?
  • Are you looking for a solution that integrates with your existing systems like library management software, campus cards, or accounting platforms?
  • Do you need secure access control for MFPs or computers, such as user authentication or time management?
  • Do you need secure access control for MFPs or computers, such as user authentication or time management?

Visitor Management

  • Are your customers looking for an on-premises or cloud solution?
  • Do you need a digital directory to guide visitors/citiznes/patrons?
  • Do you need to have two way conversations with a receptionist?
  • Do you need to deter unwanted visitors from entering your buildings?
  • Do you need to monitor entrances and exits?
  • How do you currently check in visitors?
  • Does your school have a strategic plan for physical security in case of an active shooter or emergency lockdown situation? (School Gate Guardian)
  • Does your school need the ability to attach court or custody documents to their visitor management system? (School Gate Guardian)
  • Does your school need to background check visitors? (School Gate Guardian)
  • Does your school need to report who was in the school? (School Gate Guardian)
  • Does your school need an efficient way of keeping track of visitors, parents and other people that enter your school or campus? (School Gate Guardian)

Printing/Production

  • Does your company do any marketing mass mailing?
  • Where and how do you print company invoices? Do you print a lot?
  • Do you email invoices and POs?
  • Does your company still use any large line or dot matrix printers?
  • Do you use any pre-printed forms?
